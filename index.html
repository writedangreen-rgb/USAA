USAA's Eligibility-Constrained Media Math

USAA's marketing reality is unique: a defined, eligibility-bounded TAM (~55M military-affiliated Americans) where ~25% of every untargeted impression hits an existing member. Member suppression and frequency control aren't optimizations — they're table stakes for media efficiency.

The CPM represents how efficiently you're buying media. Lower CPMs mean more impressions for your budget; premium CTV and military-endemic placements run higher.

💡 USAA Context: Per Sasi Telluri's "Reimagining Marketing Measurement" framework, the right starting question is "Right audiences to target with optimal frequency." This page sets that foundation.

Why this matters for ROMI:

  • Budget and CPM determine your raw impression volume
  • Member suppression recovers waste — every impression saved goes to a true prospect
  • Identity-based frequency caps work where cookie-based caps fail (military households cluster on/near bases)
  • Quality of impression delivery directly drives Quality Funnel Starts downstream

From Impressions to Site Visits: The Two Visitor Pools

These metrics measure how effectively your impressions drive prospective shoppers to visit your site and begin a quote.

The Media Click & View-Through Rate (CVTR) captures the combined percentage of impressions that result in a site visit — both direct ad clicks and view-through visits where someone saw the ad and later navigated to your site. This is the true media-driven visit rate for your spend.

💡 USAA Context: Combined click and view-through rates for finserv display campaigns typically range from 0.05%–0.15% for standard placements; USAA brand-trust and military-endemic placements typically index higher. View-through attribution typically accounts for 40–60% of total media-driven site visits.

Two visitor pools drive your quote funnel:

  • Media-driven visitors: Impressions × CVTR — people who visited after seeing your ad (click or view-through)
  • Organic visitors: People who arrive via search, direct, or referral — entered in the field below

Both pools flow through the same quote funnel. Combined, they determine your total new-to-USAA households and ROMI.

The USAA Quote-to-Bind Funnel

The quote-to-bind funnel measures how efficiently site visitors convert into new-to-USAA households. Both media-driven and organic visitors flow through this same funnel — and retargeting recovers a significant portion who don't convert on the first visit.

💡 The Retargeting Advantage: Without Semcasting, carriers can only identify and retarget ~35% of site visitors. With Semcasting's deterministic identity graph, that jumps to ~75% — meaning 40% more of your in-market visitors can be brought back to complete a quote and bind a policy.

Direct funnel stages:

  • Visitor to Quote Start Rate: % of all site visitors who begin entering their information
  • Quote Completion Rate: % of quote starters who complete all required fields and receive a rate
  • Policy Bind Rate: % of completed quotes that result in a purchased policy

Retargeting funnel (applied to non-converters):

  • Retarget CVTR: Combined click + view-through rate on retargeting ads served to identified non-converters. Higher than prospecting CVTR because audiences are warm and already quote-familiar.
  • Retarget Bind Rate: % of retargeted visitors who return and bind a policy. Set at or above the direct bind rate — these shoppers already received a quote and just need a nudge.

CLTV, PLTV & Bundle Economics

USAA's stated measurement framework explicitly tracks CLTV (Customer Lifetime Value) and PLTV (Policy Lifetime Value) — and per the Results section of Sasi Telluri's framework, USAA reports "sequential growth in CLTV and PLTV measures YoY across major product lines."

📊 LTV Anchors by Scenario

• Auto: ~$11,280 ($2,256 annual premium × 5 yr avg lifespan)
• Multi-Product Bundle (Auto + Home + Bank): ~$32,000 with retention uplift

Why bundle economics matter for ROMI:

  • Bundled households retain 2–3× longer than single-product — directly extending PLTV.
  • Cross-LOB cross-sell drives higher CAC efficiency — one acquisition, multiple revenue streams.
  • USAA's "Hero products" + "curated onboarding journeys" strategy is explicitly built to maximize bundle take-rate on new-to-USAA households.
  • Identity is the unlock: bundling requires knowing the household — across products, across devices, across the family. That's exactly what Semcasting's deterministic graph delivers.

Measurement Lift: Identity-based optimization typically delivers 10–15% improvement in campaign performance via better targeting, reduced waste, and stronger Quality Funnel Starts entering the conversion path.

The Semcasting Identity Advantage

The core value of Semcasting's identity resolution is simple: more of your quote abandoners become retargetable prospects.

Without advanced identity resolution, carriers can only identify and retarget about 35% of quote abandoners. That means 65% of in-market shoppers — people who started a quote and left — simply disappear.

With Semcasting's deterministic household identity graph, that identification rate rises to 75%. That additional 40% represents warm, intent-demonstrated prospects who can now be retargeted to complete their purchase.

📊 The Math: Retargeting in this model is applied only to quote abandoners (visitors who started a quote but didn't bind). It is not applied to every site visitor — that would overstate retarget conversion. This is the conservative, defensible approach.

Five sources of incremental value:

  • Better Targeting: Semcasting Precision Audience reaches more high-quality, eligibility-aware prospects per impression — additional site visitors who flow through the funnel
  • Measurement-Driven Reallocation: Pipeline-measured user-journey signal lets USAA shift budget to publishers and placements actually driving bound households — raising effective CTR per impression dollar
  • Recovered Impressions: Member suppression and frequency control redirect wasted impressions to true prospects
  • Incremental Direct Households: Additional new-to-USAA households from those recovered impressions flowing through the direct funnel
  • Retargeting Lift: The additional 40% of quote abandoners now identified — brought back via retargeting to bind
